Features

Everything your stockvel
deserves

We've taken the trust and discipline of traditional stokvels and made them more powerful with technology.

Full transparency
Every rand is tracked and visible to all members. No more disputes about who paid and when.
Automated payouts
Funds are automatically released to the right member on payout day. No treasurer stress.
Multiple payment methods
Pay via Instant EFT, debit card, or SnapScan. We support Ozow and Peach Payments.
WhatsApp reminders
Members get payment reminders on WhatsApp — no app download needed to stay updated.
Your financial identity
Build a verified savings record. Use your contribution history to access credit and insurance.
Group buying power
Access bulk discounts from partner retailers when your group shops together through Eunilite.
How it works

under 5 minutes

3

4

The right member gets paid on the right day. No manual transfers. No delays. No drama.

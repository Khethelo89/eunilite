# Eunilite — Feature Specifications

## MVP Features (Phase 1)

### Group Management
- Create a stockvel group with name, type, contribution amount, payout day
- Set group rules (late penalty, grace period, approval required)
- Generate a shareable invite link
- View all group members and their status
- Treasurer role with elevated permissions

### Member Management
- Join a group via invite link
- Member profile with name, phone, bank details
- View your turn order and payout date
- View all other members and their payout order

### Contribution Tracking
- Record a contribution (manual confirmation in Phase 1)
- View contribution history per member
- View group-wide contribution status for current month
- Late payment flagging and notifications
- Monthly contribution reminders

### Payout Schedule
- Auto-generated payout schedule based on member order
- View full cycle schedule
- Track completed vs upcoming payouts
- Payout confirmation flow for treasurer

### Notifications
- Payment due reminders (3 days before)
- Overdue payment alerts
- Payout confirmation to recipient
- New member joined notification
- WhatsApp + push + email

### Treasurer Dashboard
- Monthly collection overview (paid vs outstanding)
- One-click payment reminders to overdue members
- Payout confirmation and processing
- Group rules management
- Basic reporting

---

## Phase 2 Features

### Payments
- Ozow instant EFT
- Debit card (Visa/Mastercard)
- SnapScan QR code
- Automated debit orders
- Instant bank payouts on payout day
- Payment receipts with reference numbers

### Dispute Management
- Flag a disputed payment
- Treasurer review and resolution flow
- Audit trail for all transactions

---

## Phase 3 Features

### Savings Credit Score
- Score based on contribution consistency
- Available to share with lenders and insurers
- Exportable as a verified savings certificate

### WhatsApp Bot
- Check contribution status via WhatsApp
- Receive payout schedule
- Pay contribution via WhatsApp (Phase 3+)
- No app download required

### Group Buying
- Partner retailer discounts for group members
- Bulk purchase coordination
- Shared order tracking

### Investment Option
- Idle funds placed in money market while pooled
- Group earns interest between collection and payout
- Transparent interest reporting
